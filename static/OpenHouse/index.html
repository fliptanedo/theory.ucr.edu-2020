Fall 2017 Open House

Saturday, October 28, 2017; 1:00 pm - 3:30 pm; part of UCR Discover Day

Discover UCR Physics & Astro

You and your family are invited to an afternoon of physics fun! Come meet the faculty, students, staff, and friends of the UCR Department of Physics and Astronomy. All are welcome!

  • Free UCR Physics & Astronomy t-shirts and electric motor kits!
    (Available while supplies last.)
  • Fun, hands-on physics demonstrations with faculty and students.
  • Ask a physicist about cutting-edge research at UCR.
  • Tours of research laboratories

Information for prospective beginning and transfer students will be available. Come find out what makes UC Riverside a special place to study physics & astronomy.

The Physics & Open House is on the same day as UCR Discover Day. Visitors attending Discover Day are very welcome to join us for the Open House, but you are not expected or required to attend both.
